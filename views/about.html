About

Hi there!

My name's Taylor and I'm the owner of Tortie Designs. This is Zelda - she's my "assistant".

Tortie Designs was started in April 2017 when I noticed my tortoiseshell kitten (Zelda) playing with one of my handmade polymer clay necklaces. I had recently started using polymer clay as an expressive medium for jewelry, and I ended up developing a brand inspired by the well known attitude of Tortiseshell cats - "tortitude" - cute, bold, but still subdue - with emphasis on keeping things bright and statement, but practical enough for everyday wear at the same time - not to mention, glitter wherever possible.

We hope you enjoy Tortie Designs' creations.

Taylor & Zelda
