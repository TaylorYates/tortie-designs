Wholesale

We love wholesaling! Within the first few months of operation (we opened in April 2017!) we're due to appear in nearly 10 stores Australia wide. If you're interested in stocking our brand, make sure you check out our About section and then send us an email here.

We have no minimum order - so if you want to order 1 - 3 pieces to try out in your store, that is more than welcome. Please contact us for wholesale pricing.

We will also be visiting the USA in October 2017. If you are in the USA and would like to stock our brand, contact us us before September 2017 - and we might be able to bring it with us!

Please note that wholesale items will incur a shipping cost. This is based on weight. All wholesale items in Australia are sent by Sendle. For more information on our terms and conditions, send us an email.

Unfortunately, as we are the micro-est of micro-businesses, we can not offer consignment or drop-shipping arrangements.
